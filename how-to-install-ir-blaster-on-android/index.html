Types of IR Blasters

There are two types of ir blasters available: external ir blasters and built-in ir blasters.

An external ir blaster is a small device that can be connected to the headphone jack of your device and is compatible with most Android devices. It comes with an app that you can download and use to control your TV and other home appliances from a distance.
