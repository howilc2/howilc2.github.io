How to Install Plex on Kodi

Installing the Plex Kodi extension is not as complicated as you might think. There are several methods to do this, but we will cover the most straightforward method. Here’s how:

  1. Launch Kodi and click on the “Add-ons” tab.
  2. Select “Install from Repository.”
  3. Choose Kodi Add-on Repository and select Video Add-ons.
  4. Scroll down and click on “Plex” and then click “Install.”
  5. Wait for the installation to complete. Once done, you’ll receive a notification telling you that the Plex add-on has been installed.
  6. Now, go back to the Kodi home screen and click on “Add-ons” again. Hover over “Video Add-ons” and click on “Plex.”
  7. Login with your Plex account and enjoy your media content!

How to Connect Your Plex Account to Kodi

Connecting your Plex account to Kodi is essential if you want to enjoy the best media experience possible. Here’s how:

  1. Launch the Plex app on Kodi and click on the settings icon.
  2. Select “Sign in to Plex.”
  3. Head to plex.tv/link and enter the six-character code provided by the Plex app.
  4. Sign in to your Plex account and complete the authentication process.
  5. Once authenticated, your Plex account will be linked to Kodi.
