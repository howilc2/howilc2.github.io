How to Format Your SD Card for NOOBS

To start the process of installing NOOBS on your SD card, you’ll need to format the card first. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Insert your SD card into your computer’s SD card reader.
  2. Open your computer’s file explorer and right-click on the SD card.
  3. Select “Format.”
  4. In the format window, select “FAT32” as the file system and click “Start.”
  5. Wait for the formatting process to complete.

Once your SD card is formatted, you’re ready to move on to the next step.

Copying NOOBS Files to Your SD Card

Once you’ve downloaded the NOOBS software and formatted your SD card, it’s time to copy the NOOBS files to the card. Here’s how:

  1. Open the NOOBS zip file you downloaded earlier.
  2. Select all of the files and folders inside the zip file.
  3. Copy the files and folders to the root directory of your SD card.

Once the files have finished copying over, you’re ready to insert your SD card into your Raspberry Pi and start the installation process.

Installing NOOBS on Your Raspberry Pi

Insert the SD card into your Raspberry Pi and power it on. Make sure your Raspberry Pi is connected to your monitor, keyboard, and mouse. After a minute or two, the NOOBS installer screen will appear. Follow the on-screen instructions to select the operating system you want to install, and the installation process will begin.
