How to Install TV Addons in Kodi

Installing TV addons on Kodi is quick and easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Launch Kodi and click on the “Settings” icon on the top left side of the home screen.
  2. Select “System Settings” and then click on “Add-ons.”
  3. Make sure “Unknown sources” is enabled, and click “Yes” when the warning prompt appears.
  4. Go back to the home screen and click on the “Settings” icon once again.
  5. Select “File Manager” and then click on “Add source.”
  6. Enter the following URL in the box: http://fusion.tvaddons.co
  7. Name the source “TV Addons” and click “OK.”
  8. Go back to the home screen and click on “Add-ons.”
  9. Select the “Package Installer” icon in the top left corner.
  10. Click on “Install from zip file” and select “TV Addons.”
  11. Choose “kodi-repos” and then “English.”
  12. Select “repository.xbmchub-x.x.x.zip” and wait for the add-on enabled notification.
  13. Select “Install from repository” and choose “TVADDONS.CO Add-on Repository.”
  14. Select the add-on you want to install and click “Install.”
  15. Wait for the add-on enabled notification, and you’re done!
