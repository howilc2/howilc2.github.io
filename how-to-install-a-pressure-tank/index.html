Choosing the Right Size of Pressure Tank

The size of the pressure tank you choose depends on several factors, including the size of your home and the number of people living in it. As a general rule of thumb, a 20-gallon tank is sufficient for a home with two to three people, while a 40-gallon tank is better for larger homes or families.

Types of Pressure Tanks

There are two main types of pressure tanks: diaphragm and bladder tanks. Diaphragm tanks have a rubber membrane that separates air and water, while bladder tanks have a bag that separates air and water. Both types are effective, but bladder tanks tend to be more efficient because they require less maintenance.
