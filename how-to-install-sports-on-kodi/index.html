How to Install Sports on Kodi

Installing sports on Kodi is a simple process that can be done in a few easy steps. The first thing to do is to launch Kodi and click on the gear icon to access the settings. Next, click on “System” and then “Add-ons.” Enable “Unknown Sources” to allow third-party add-ons to install. Click “Yes” in the warning message that appears.

Go back to the previous menu and click on “File Manager.” Click on “Add source” and enter the URL of the repository. Click “OK” to continue. Click on “Install from Zip file” and select the repository you just added. Click “OK” to continue.

Click on “Install from repository” and select the add-on you want to install. Click “Install” and wait for the add-on to complete its installation process. Click “OK” when the installation is complete. You can now access the add-on from the main menu and enjoy your favorite live sports action.
