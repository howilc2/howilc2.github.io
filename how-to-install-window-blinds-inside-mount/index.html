If you’re like most homeowners, you probably want to create a modern, neat, and relaxed vibe in every room in your home. However, nothing can disrupt that vibe more than a window treatment that doesn’t fit or work correctly. When it comes to installing window blinds, it can be pretty challenging to ensure that you get it right the first time. But don’t worry, we’re here to help you with that process. In this article, we’ll guide you through the steps on how to install window blinds inside mount correctly.

Challenges faced while installing window blinds inside mount

Even if we try our best with a DIY project, there are always some unforeseen challenges that pop up along the way. The same goes for installing window blinds inside mount. Some of these challenges may include not accurately measuring the window, buying the wrong sized blinds, and not having the right tools. These challenges can make the process more complicated and lead to frustration.

How to install window blinds inside mount?

To install window blinds inside mount, you’re going to need a few tools such as a drill, screws, and brackets that fit your blinds, and a pencil for marking. Firstly, you need to measure the exact width of your window from inside the frame to install the brackets that hold your blinds. After that, mark where your brackets should be attached on your window. Then, attach your brackets using the screws and a driller. Finally, after attaching the brackets, snap your window blinds in place. That’s it. Your blinds are up.

Summary of how to install window blinds inside mount

To recap, before installing window blinds inside mount, ensure you have the correct tools and measurements. Measure the exact width of your window from inside the frame, mark where your brackets should be attached, attach your brackets using your driller, and snap your window blinds in place.

Benefits of installing window blinds inside mount

Installing window blinds inside mount offers benefits such as a cleaner and more polished look for your windows. This installation method eliminates unsightly gaps between the window trim and shades. Additionally, inside mount shades provide a great solution for windows that don’t have enough depth for outside mount installations.

How to choose the right blind for inside mount installations?

When it comes to picking the right blind for inside mount installations, it’s essential to choose the correct sized blinds that fit your window. You should check the width, height, and depth of your window to ensure that you buy the correct sized blinds. Measure twice for accuracy. Also, consider the material of the blinds. Faux wood is often an excellent choice since it’s moisture-resistant and offers a great look for your inside mount installation.

Things to avoid when installing window blinds inside mount

When installing window blinds inside mount, it’s essential to avoid a few things such as forcing the window blinds into a too-tight space or not securing your brackets tightly. Ensure that the brackets are spaced evenly, or the blinds will not hang straight.

Question and Answer Section

Q1. How much time does it typically take to install window blinds inside mount?

A1. On average, it takes about 30 minutes to an hour per window to install window blinds inside mount. This time may vary depending on how many blinds you need to install and the size of your windows.

Q2. Is it advisable to hire a professional to install window blinds inside mount?

A2. It’s not necessary to hire a professional if you’ve got basic DIY skills. However, if you don’t feel comfortable installing the blinds yourself or don’t have the necessary tools, hiring a professional may be best.

Q3. What’s the average price range for window blinds?

A3. Window blinds come in different price ranges, depending on the type, material, and brand. However, the average price range of window blinds is between $25 to $100 per window, but some can cost up to $500 per window.

Q4. How do I maintain the window blinds after installation?

A4. To maintain your window blinds properly, dust or vacuum them regularly. Use a damp cloth or sponge to clean them when necessary, avoid using harsh cleaning solutions and allow them to dry thoroughly before opening your windows.

Conclusion of how to install window blinds inside mount

In conclusion, installing window blinds inside mount wouldn’t be as challenging if you had the proper tools, measurements, and a little bit of patience. Follow the steps carefully, and you’ll have new window blinds up in no time. These tips and tricks offered in this guide will make sure you get the perfect fit for your inside mount window blinds, ensuring your windows boast a clean, sophisticated look that compliments your home’s aesthetics.
