Step-by-Step Guide to Install Sim Card in iPhone 7

1. Turn off your iPhone before starting the process. This will prevent any accidental damage to your phone or your sim card.

2. Locate the sim card tray on the side of your iPhone 7. You can use the sim card ejector tool to unlock the tray. Simply insert the tool into the small hole next to the tray and push gently. The tray should pop out.

3. Remove the old sim card from the tray and carefully insert the new sim card. Make sure the sim card is properly aligned and fits securely in the tray.

4. Carefully insert the tray back into your iPhone 7. Make sure it clicks into place and is securely locked.

5. Turn on your iPhone 7 and wait for it to detect the new sim card. You may need to enter your sim card PIN, which is the four-digit code provided by your carrier.
