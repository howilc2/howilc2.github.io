Are you tired of washing dishes by hand? Do you want to upgrade to a dishwasher but are unsure of the cost to have it installed? Look no further than Lowe’s! In this article, we will answer the burning question: how much does Lowes charge to install a dishwasher?

Pain Points

One of the major pain points when it comes to installing a dishwasher is the cost. Many homeowners opt to install it themselves to save money, but the process can be complicated and time-consuming. Additionally, if not installed correctly, it can lead to leaks and damage to your cabinets and flooring.

The Answer

The price to install a dishwasher at Lowe’s varies depending on the complexity of the installation. The installation fee starts at $149 which includes disconnecting and removing the old unit, connecting the new dishwasher to existing water, power and drainage lines, and testing the new dishwasher for proper operation. An additional fee may be charged for things like custom cabinetry modifications or electrical work.

Summary of Main Points

Lowes charges a starting fee of $149 for dishwasher installation which includes removal of your old dishwasher and connecting the new one to existing connections. The price may vary depending on additional work required.

Personal Experience

When I upgraded to a new dishwasher, I decided to hire Lowe’s for the installation. I was impressed with their customer service and promptness. The installer walked me through the installation process and made sure that everything was working correctly before he left. The total cost for my installation was $189 but it was worth it for the peace of mind and professional installation.

Installation Process

The installation process typically takes around 2-4 hours depending on the complexity of the job. The installer will begin by disconnecting and removing your old dishwasher. Next, they will install the mounting brackets and connect the water, power, and drainage lines to the new dishwasher. Once everything is connected, they will test the dishwasher for leaks and proper operation. Finally, they will secure the dishwasher in place and make any necessary adjustments to ensure a perfect fit.

Warranty and Guarantees

Lowes offers a one-year warranty on all installation services, which covers any defects or issues related to the installation itself. Additionally, if you have any problems with your newly installed dishwasher, they offer a 90-day satisfaction guarantee where they will come back and fix any issues free of charge.

FAQs

1. Do I need to purchase my dishwasher from Lowe’s to use their installation services?

No, Lowe’s offers installation services for both their own products and products purchased elsewhere.

2. Can I install my dishwasher myself?

You can, but it is not recommended unless you have experience and knowledge of plumbing and electrical work. Incorrect installation can lead to leaks and damage to your home.

3. Does Lowe’s offer installation for other appliances?

Yes, Lowe’s offers installation services for a variety of appliances including refrigerators, ranges, and washing machines.

4. Will the installer dispose of my old dishwasher?

Yes, the installer will remove and dispose of your old dishwasher as part of the installation process.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the cost to have Lowe’s install your dishwasher starts at $149 and includes removal of your old dishwasher and installation of the new one. Additional fees may apply for custom cabinetry modifications or electrical work. Lowe’s offers a warranty and guarantees their installation services for 90 days. Hiring a professional installation service can save you time, money, and the headache of a DIY job gone wrong.
